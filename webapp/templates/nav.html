GB Names ×

Welcome to the GB Names website. Our research, carried out at the ESRC Consumer Data Research Centre and the UCL Urban Dynamics Lab, is exploring the spread of family names across Great Britain, using historic censuses and recent consumer registers.
What's your family name?
GB Names records surname searches for statistical purposes. Surname searches that have not been previously executed by you or by others may take up to 30 seconds.

About GB Names ×

GB Names focuses upon the origins and spread of family bloodlines through surnames as manifest in the past and over more recent years.
